OUR STORY

Redefining Beauty Standards

Founded in 2010, Niro Salon began as a small boutique studio with a big vision: to create a space where beauty meets artistry, and every client feels valued and understood.

Over the years, we've grown into a premier beauty destination, known for our innovative techniques, commitment to education, and personalized approach to each client's unique needs.
